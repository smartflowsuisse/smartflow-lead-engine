# Construction Automation Starter — Sales Playbook

## Goal

Use the Construction Workflow Templates page as a sales tool for Swiss construction companies.

Main offer:

- Invoice PDF Automation
- Project Task Workflow
- Procurement Weekly Report
- Client Delivery Pack
- Implementation Checklist
- Monthly support retainer

Offer range:

- CHF 6'000–12'000 + monthly support retainer

## Best-fit clients

Target companies:

- Swiss construction companies
- Renovation companies
- Building service companies
- Small project-based contractors
- Companies handling invoices, site follow-ups, purchasing, and weekly reporting manually

Good signs:

- many PDF invoices
- manual Excel tracking
- unclear project follow-up
- repeated supplier documents
- weekly reporting by email
- missed tasks or delayed approvals
- no central workflow

Avoid at first:

- companies with no clear admin process
- companies unwilling to share sample documents
- companies only asking for a website
- companies with no budget for process improvement

## First outreach message

Hello,

I noticed that many construction companies lose time on invoices, project follow-ups, procurement tracking, and weekly reporting.

SmartFlow Suisse has prepared a Construction Automation Starter with ready-to-demo workflows for:

- invoice PDF extraction
- project task follow-ups
- procurement reports

We can offer a free workflow audit and show where automation could save time before proposing any implementation.

Would it be useful to review one of your current invoice or project follow-up workflows?

## Discovery questions

Ask these questions before proposing implementation:

1. How do you currently receive supplier invoices?
2. Who reviews and approves invoices?
3. Do you track project costs manually?
4. How do you follow up open project tasks?
5. Do you prepare weekly purchasing or project reports?
6. Which tools do you use today?
7. Where do delays usually happen?
8. Which process takes the most admin time every week?
9. Who should receive alerts or reports?
10. Can you share 2–3 sample documents for a workflow audit?

## Client intake checklist

Ask the client to prepare:

- sample invoices
- supplier documents
- project notes
- purchase lists
- weekly reports
- current Excel/CRM/accounting setup
- approval rules
- responsible team members
- main pain points

## Sales flow

Step 1: Identify construction lead  
Step 2: Open lead details  
Step 3: Check website and contact data  
Step 4: Send audit message  
Step 5: Offer free workflow audit  
Step 6: Collect sample documents  
Step 7: Prepare proposal summary  
Step 8: Propose CHF 6'000–12'000 + retainer  
Step 9: Move qualified lead to Pipeline  
Step 10: Start implementation checklist

## When to propose the paid package

Only propose the paid package when:

- the client has a real manual workflow
- the client confirms pain or lost time
- the client can share documents
- the workflow can be automated in 1–5 days
- there is a clear business owner or admin contact

## Retainer logic

Monthly support can include:

- workflow monitoring
- small fixes
- template updates
- reporting review
- improvement suggestions
- support for new document formats

Do not include:

- full redesign
- unrelated website work
- unlimited new automations
- complex ERP migration
- custom accounting replacement

## Internal rule

Do not sell automation before understanding the real workflow.

First sell clarity:
- audit
- sample documents
- workflow map
- small implementation

Then sell automation.
