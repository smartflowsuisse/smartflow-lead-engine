# SmartFlow Demo Script Draft

## Purpose

This script helps present the SmartFlow demo to a first potential client.

The goal is to explain the value clearly, show a safe workflow, and avoid overpromising.

## Demo principle

The demo should be simple:

- show the client problem;
- show the intake;
- show the CRM-style structure;
- show the AI summary;
- show human review;
- propose one safe next step.

Do not turn the demo into a technical lecture.

## Opening

Suggested opening:

Bonjour, merci pour votre temps.

L'objectif aujourd'hui n'est pas de vous vendre une grande solution compliquée.

L'objectif est de vous montrer comment SmartFlow peut aider une PME à mieux suivre les demandes, réduire les tâches répétitives et garder un contrôle humain sur les actions importantes.

## Step 1 — Confirm the business problem

Explain:

Many small companies lose time because requests arrive from different places:

- website form;
- email;
- phone;
- referrals;
- manual notes;
- Excel or Google Sheets.

The risk is not only lost time.

The real risk is:

- missed follow-ups;
- delayed replies;
- unclear priorities;
- repeated manual work;
- no clear view of open opportunities.

## Step 2 — Show intake

Show one safe demo request.

Possible example:

- a Google Form submission;
- a manual lead entry;
- a website contact request;
- a test company in the CRM demo sheet.

Say clearly:

This is demo data. We do not need to expose private client data to demonstrate the workflow.

## Step 3 — Show CRM-style structure

Show where the request is stored.

Point out:

- company;
- contact person;
- email;
- phone;
- website;
- source;
- language;
- status;
- priority;
- notes;
- next action.

Explain:

The goal is not to create a complex CRM. The goal is to create a simple, reliable operating view.

## Step 4 — Show AI summary

Show how AI can summarize the request.

Explain that the AI can help identify:

- what the client needs;
- what the likely problem is;
- what information is missing;
- what next action may be useful.

Important phrase:

The AI does not decide alone. It prepares a draft for human review.

## Step 5 — Show human approval

Explain the control layer:

Before any external action, a human reviews:

- the company;
- the contact data;
- the AI summary;
- the proposed answer;
- whether sending is appropriate.

Safety message:

SmartFlow does not need to send automatic emails in the first version. It can prepare a draft and let the team approve it manually.

## Step 6 — Show possible next action

Allowed demo outputs:

- update CRM status;
- create a note;
- create a follow-up task;
- prepare a draft reply;
- recommend the next manual action.

Do not show:

- automatic mass outreach;
- automatic external sending;
- unverified client data;
- production changes during the demo.

## Step 7 — Connect to business value

Explain the value in practical terms:

This kind of workflow can help your team:

- answer faster;
- avoid forgotten requests;
- keep a clear follow-up list;
- reduce repetitive admin work;
- understand which requests need attention first.

Do not promise exact time savings unless measured.

## Step 8 — Ask discovery questions

Ask:

- How do new requests arrive today?
- Who answers them?
- Where are they tracked?
- What gets delayed most often?
- What is repeated every week?
- Who must approve outgoing messages?
- What data must remain private?
- What would make this useful in the first month?

## Step 9 — Propose one next step

Offer one clear next step:

- a paid workflow audit;
- a small automation quick win;
- a CRM/contact-form cleanup;
- a follow-up proposal.

Recommended phrase:

The safest next step would be to review your current workflow first, then propose one small improvement that can be implemented without disrupting your existing process.

## What not to say

Do not say:

- everything will be fully automated;
- AI will replace your team;
- we can connect everything immediately;
- this will save a fixed number of hours without analysis;
- automatic emails will be sent without review;
- the setup is risk-free.

## Safety reminders

- No automatic external email sending.
- No mass outreach.
- Human approval required.
- No production changes during demo.
- No sensitive data exposure.
- No overpromising.
- Demo data preferred.

## Good demo outcome

A good demo is successful if the client understands:

- what problem SmartFlow solves;
- what part can be automated;
- what part stays human-controlled;
- what a safe first step looks like;
- why a small audit or quick win is better than a large rushed project.
