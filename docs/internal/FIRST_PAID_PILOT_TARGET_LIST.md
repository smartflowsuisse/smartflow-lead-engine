# SmartFlow First Paid Pilot Target List

## Purpose

This document defines the first short target list for a paid SmartFlow Lead & Follow-up Pilot.

The goal is not mass outreach.

The goal is to prepare a small number of carefully reviewed manual approaches for companies that are likely to have request-tracking, follow-up, or lead-management pain.

## Offer to connect

Primary offer:

SmartFlow Lead & Follow-up Pilot

Core value:

- centralize incoming requests;
- improve follow-up visibility;
- prepare internal AI summaries;
- define the next manual action;
- keep the human in control.

## Selection rules

Use only leads that match most of the following:

- construction, renovation, menuiserie, real estate, home services, or local service business;
- Suisse romande preferred;
- website exists;
- email, phone, or contact page exists;
- lead score is strong enough;
- request-tracking or follow-up pain is plausible;
- no mass outreach;
- no automatic email;
- each approach must be reviewed manually.

## First manual preparation checklist

Before contacting any company:

- confirm website still opens;
- confirm contact page;
- confirm email or contact form;
- check CRM status;
- check outreach status;
- check activity history;
- prepare one company-specific reason;
- prepare French wording manually;
- confirm no duplicate contact;
- confirm no automatic email will be sent.
