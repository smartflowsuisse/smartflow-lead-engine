# SmartFlow French Discovery Email Templates

## Purpose

This document contains internal French email templates for first-client readiness.

These templates are for manual use only.

They must not be used for automatic mass outreach.

## Core rules

- Manual review required before sending.
- No automatic external email sending.
- No mass outreach.
- No unverified recipient addresses.
- No overpromising.
- No fake claims about results, savings, or clients.
- Adapt each message to the company before sending.

## Tone

Use a tone that is:

- professional;
- short;
- clear;
- respectful;
- not pushy;
- suitable for Swiss SMEs.

Avoid:

- aggressive sales language;
- exaggerated AI claims;
- long explanations;
- technical overload.

## Template 1 — First contact after website review

- Petite suggestion pour votre suivi client
- Amélioration simple de votre suivi des demandes
- Idée rapide pour mieux gérer les demandes entrantes

Si cela vous intéresse, je peux vous proposer un court audit de votre processus actuel et identifier une amélioration concrète et réaliste.

Meilleures salutations,

SmartFlow Suisse

## Template 10 — Internal manual checklist before sending

Before sending any message manually, check:

- company name is correct;
- email address is verified;
- language is appropriate;
- message is adapted to the company;
- no false claim is included;
- no specific savings are promised without analysis;
- no automatic sending is used;
- no mass outreach is involved;
- follow-up is logged manually if needed.
