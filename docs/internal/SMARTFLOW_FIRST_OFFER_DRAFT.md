# SmartFlow Suisse — First Offer Draft

## 1. Positioning

SmartFlow Suisse helps small and medium businesses in Suisse romande reduce repetitive administrative work with simple AI-assisted workflows, automation, and clear client follow-up systems.

We do not start by replacing the client's tools.

We first review the current workflow, identify where time is lost, and propose 1 or 2 simple improvements that can be tested safely.

## 2. Target clients

Initial focus:

- construction companies;
- renovation and home services;
- local service businesses;
- PME with incoming requests, documents, emails, quotes, and follow-ups.

Best-fit client:

- receives requests by email, website form, phone, or PDF;
- loses time on manual follow-ups;
- has no clear CRM or uses spreadsheets manually;
- wants practical improvement, not a complex software project.

## 3. Client pain

Typical problems:

- incoming requests are not tracked clearly;
- follow-ups are forgotten;
- documents and emails are handled manually;
- quotes or project requests are spread across inboxes;
- owner or admin staff spends time on repetitive tasks;
- there is no simple view of New / Contacted / Follow-up / Won / Lost;
- AI is interesting, but the client does not know where to use it safely.

## 4. First entry offer — Free Workflow Audit

Working name:

**Audit gratuit de votre suivi administratif**

Purpose:

Review how the company handles incoming requests, follow-ups, documents, and repetitive administrative tasks.

The goal is to identify 1 or 2 realistic automation opportunities without changing the client's existing tools.

Included:

- short review of website/contact process;
- review of incoming request handling;
- review of follow-up process;
- identification of repetitive admin tasks;
- 1-page summary with quick wins;
- recommendation of one simple next step.

Not included:

- full website redesign;
- full CRM implementation;
- complex software development;
- guaranteed revenue increase;
- automated email sending;
- access to sensitive data without approval.

## 5. Paid offer 1 — Automation Quick Win

Working name:

**Automation Quick Win**

Purpose:

Implement one small, practical workflow that saves time.

Examples:

- contact form → Google Sheets CRM;
- email/request → structured lead row;
- request → AI draft prepared for human review;
- lead → follow-up task;
- weekly summary of open requests;
- PDF/document intake → structured checklist.

Estimated price range:

- 1,200–5,000 CHF depending on scope.

Delivery:

- usually 3–10 working days for a small workflow;
- client approval before connecting real tools;
- manual review remains in place.

## 6. Paid offer 2 — Foundation Web + Workflow

Purpose:

For clients who need both a stronger website/contact flow and a simple internal workflow.

Included:

- website/contact flow review;
- improved contact/request capture;
- simple CRM or Google Sheets workflow;
- AI-assisted draft process;
- follow-up process;
- basic analytics and tracking;
- documentation.

Estimated price range:

- 3,500–7,500 CHF depending on scope.

## 7. Monthly support — SmartFlow Care

Purpose:

Maintain and improve the client's workflow after launch.

Included:

- monthly workflow check;
- small text/process updates;
- form/email checks;
- minor improvements;
- simple report;
- support for questions.

Estimated starting price:

- from 149 CHF/month for basic support;
- higher for automation monitoring or active workflow support.

## 8. First sales message

Core message in simple language:

> Nous aidons les PME à réduire le temps perdu sur les tâches répétitives: demandes entrantes, relances, documents PDF, suivi client et préparation administrative. Nous commençons par un court audit gratuit pour identifier 1 ou 2 automatisations simples, sans changer vos outils existants.

## 9. Safety principles

Rules:

- no mass outreach;
- no automated sending without approval;
- no sensitive client data in AI tools without review;
- no risky access changes without confirmation;
- human validates every client-facing message;
- backups before important internal data changes;
- simple tools first: Google Sheets, Make, Docs.

## 10. Recommended first client path

1. First contact.
2. Short reply or call.
3. Free workflow audit.
4. Mini-audit PDF.
5. Proposal for one Quick Win.
6. Implementation.
7. Documentation.
8. Optional monthly support.

## 11. What we should not sell first

Do not lead with:

- public SaaS;
- complex AI platform;
- full CRM migration;
- large transformation project;
- vague AI consulting;
- SEO-only offer;
- bulk email automation.

Start with one concrete workflow problem.
