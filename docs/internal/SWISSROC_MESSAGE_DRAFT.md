## Preferred version

Use the shorter version first.

Reason:

- less aggressive;
- suitable for first contact;
- no unsupported claim;
- no promise;
- no pricing;
- no pressure.

## What not to include

Do not include:

- claims that Swissroc loses leads;
- claims that Swissroc has a broken process;
- claims that Swissroc responds slowly;
- technical Make/OpenAI details;
- pricing in the first message;
- attachments;
- pressure language;
- automatic follow-up wording.
