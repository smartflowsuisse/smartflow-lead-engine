## 4. Discovery call checklist

Questions to ask:

1. What is the main goal of the website today?
2. Do you receive enough requests through the website?
3. Where do requests arrive: email, phone, form, CRM?
4. Who handles incoming requests internally?
5. Are there repetitive tasks after a new request arrives?
6. Do you want more leads, better qualification, or time savings?
7. What would make this useful for you within 30 days?

## 5. Delivery format for mini-audit

Mini-audit should include:

- 1 short introduction;
- 5–7 concrete observations;
- 1–2 quick wins;
- what we would not change;
- proposed next step;
- simple price range if asked.

## 6. Boundaries

Do not promise:

- guaranteed leads;
- guaranteed ranking in Google;
- full redesign in ation without understanding their workflow;
- legal/data compliance guarantees without review.

Can promise:

- structured review;
- practical recommendations;
- small controlled implementation;
- human-supervised AI workflow;
- clear before/after scope.
