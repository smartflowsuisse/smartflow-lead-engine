# First Client Pricing and Offer Guide — SmartFlow Suisse

Purpose: internal pricing and offer guide before sending first real outreach emails.

Status: internal only. Do not send directly to clients.

---

## 1. Main rule

Do not sell a large project too early.

For first clients, the goal is:

- create trust;
- show one concrete observation;
- offer a small controlled next step;
- avoid overpromising;
- avoid unclear custom pricing too early.

---

## 2. Recommended first-client offer ladder

### Offer 1 — Short website mini-audit

Use when:
- client asks for the analysis;
- client is curious but not ready to buy;
- we want to build trust.

Scope:
- 5–7 observations;
- mobile experience;
- contact path;
- basic SEO/accessibility/performance signals;
- 1–2 quick wins;
- no full technical implementation.

Pricing:
- for first selected prospects: can be offered free;
- later: 190–290 CHF.

Internal decision:
- For the first 3–5 carefully selected prospects, mini-audit can be free if it helps start the conversation.

---

### Offer 2 — Quick Win Website Improvement

Use when:
- client wants action after mini-audit;
- issue is limited and clear;
- no full redesign is needed.

Scope examples:
- improve mobile performance basics;
- optimize large images;
- improve contact CTA visibility;
- improve contact path;
- fix small accessibility or best-practices issues;
- review form/contact flow.

Not included:
- full redesign;
- full SEO campaign;
- custom CRM build;
- large automation;
- guaranteed leads.

Pricing:
- 490–900 CHF depending on scope.

Recommended first-client positioning:
- 690 CHF fixed small package, if scope is simple and controlled.

---

### Offer 3 — Website + Lead Flow Improvement

Use when:
- client wants better handling of incoming requests;
- website/contact process is unclear;
- forms, email, CRM or follow-up can be improved.

Scope:
- contact form review;
- lead notification flow;
- basic CRM or spreadsheet structure;
- follow-up email template;
- simple tracking of requests.

Pricing:
- 900–1,500 CHF for a controlled first implementation.

---

### Offer 4 — SmartFlow Lead & Follow-up System

Use when:
- client has enough incoming requests;
- follow-up process is manual;
- there is a clear operational pain.

Scope:
- lead capture;
- CRM structure;
- AI-assisted draft replies;
- follow-up reminders;
- reporting dashboard or simple tracking.

Pricing:
- 1,500–3,500+ CHF depending on workflow.

Do not offer this before understanding the client workflow.

## 4. What to say if client asks price immediately

French response logic:

1. Do not avoid the question.
2. Give a range.
3. Explain that final price depends on scope.
4. Offer a small first step.

Suggested answer:

Bonjour,

Pour une première intervention courte, le budget dépend surtout du périmètre.

À titre indicatif :
- une amélioration simple du site ou du parcours de contact se situe souvent entre 490 et 900 CHF ;
- un travail plus complet autour du site, des demandes entrantes et du suivi peut commencer autour de 1,200–1,500 CHF ;
- un système plus avancé avec automatisation ou CRM se chiffre après avoir compris le fonctionnement interne.

Dans votre cas, je recommanderais d’abord une courte analyse concrète du site, afin d’identifier si une intervention limitée suffit.

---

## 5. What not to say

Do not say:
- “We guarantee more clients.”
- “We will rank you on Google.”
- “Your current site is bad.”
- “We can automate everything.”
- “This is easy” before checking details.

Better say:
- “There may be a simple improvement.”
- “The current site already has a solid base.”
- “We can start with a limited and controlled scope.”
- “The goal is not necessarily a redesign.”

## 7. Internal checklist before giving a price

Before giving a fixed price, confirm:

- website technology is manageable;
- access can be provided;
- scope is limited;
- no full redesign is expected;
- client understands what is included and excluded;
- no legal/data-sensitive automation is involved;
- implementation can be done safely.
