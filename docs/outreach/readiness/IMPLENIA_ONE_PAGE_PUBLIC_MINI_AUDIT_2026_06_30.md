## 4. Potential Friction Point

Possible public-facing friction point:

A visitor may need to choose between several contact paths:

- central contact;
- local branch/location contact;
- topic-specific contact;
- named contact person.

This could create a useful opportunity for a small manual-first intake and follow-up support layer.

Safe framing:

- public contact path reviewed;
- multiple visible contact entry points;
- possible structured intake opportunity;
- manual-first follow-up support;
- no automation of external replies without human review.

## 5. Lead & Follow-up Pilot Angle

Draft angle:

SmartFlow Suisse could propose a small manual-first Lead & Follow-up Pilot for public inbound contact requests.

The pilot would help:

- capture incoming requests in a structured internal format;
- summarize the request with AI;
- identify request type or topic;
- identify missing information;
- suggest a next manual action;
- prepare an internal follow-up note;
- keep a human review step before any client communication.

Important:

This is not an automated email sending system.

This is not mass outreach.

This is not a replacement for Implenia internal processes.

This is not a claim that Implenia has a broken or weak process.

This is a small controlled operational support workflow.
