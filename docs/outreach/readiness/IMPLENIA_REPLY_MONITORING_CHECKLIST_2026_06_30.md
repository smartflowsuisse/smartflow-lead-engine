## 2. Purpose of this checklist

This checklist defines what to do after the manual Implenia first-contact email.

The goal is to keep the process safe, manual, calm, and non-aggressive.

## 3. Monitoring rule

Check inbox manually.

Do not use automated reply monitoring.

Do not use Gmail automation.

Do not use Make to send or process external emails.

Do not mark anything as replied unless a real human reply is received.

## 4. If Implenia replies positively

If Implenia replies with interest:

1. Read the reply carefully.
2. Do not promise results.
3. Do not claim that SmartFlow has found an internal issue.
4. Offer to send the short one-page note.
5. Keep the response short and manual.
6. Record the reply in tracking only after the reply is actually received.

Allowed next action:

- Send the one-page note manually if requested or clearly welcomed.

## 5. If Implenia asks for the one-page note

Use the prepared public-only mini-audit.

Before sending, verify again that it contains:

- public-only observations;
- no accusation;
- no claim about lost leads;
- no claim about slow response;
- no claim about broken CRM;
- no guaranteed result;
- no pressure.

## 6. If Implenia asks for pricing

Use the first-client pricing and offer guide.

Do not invent a custom price during the reply.

Do not offer a paid pilot unless the scope is clear.

Do not mark Paid Pilot unless Implenia explicitly agrees.

## 7. If Implenia asks for a call

Use the French discovery call checklist.

The call goal should be:

- understand current intake flow;
- identify whether a small manual-first pilot is useful;
- avoid over-selling;
- avoid promising automation before understanding the process.

## 8. If there is no reply

Do nothing immediately.

Do not send a second email on the same day.

Do not start an aggressive sequence.

A maximum of one polite follow-up may be considered only after a reasonable pause.

The follow-up must be:

- short;
- polite;
- manual;
- non-accusatory;
- without pressure;
- without claims about Implenia internal problems.

## 9. Tracking discipline

Do not mark:

- Replied unless a real reply is received;
- Paid Pilot unless explicitly agreed;
- Follow-up sent unless a follow-up is actually sent;
- Call requested unless Implenia requests or accepts a call.

## 10. Explicitly forbidden

- No mass outreach.
- No automated email sending.
- No Gmail sending module.
- No Make schedule for outbound email.
- No CRM auto-update based on assumptions.
- No second email today.
- No negative claims about Implenia.
- No guaranteed outcome.
- No pressure sequence.
